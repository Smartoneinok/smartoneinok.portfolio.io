About Me

As a Senior Developer Advocate at Box, my focus is on the intersection of content and Artificial Intelligence. I empower developers by teaching them how to leverage Box APIs and cutting-edge AI technologies through engaging content, including technical blogs, video tutorials, sample code, and live demonstrations. I also take pride in mentoring team members and leading initiatives that expand our reach.

I've significantly contributed to our developer relations social media presence, creating demos that have garnered millions of impressions and interactions. A key part of my role involves collaborating with major AI partners like OpenAI, Google, Weaviate, and Pinecone to build innovative content and integrations, including developing multiple RAG (Retrieval Augmented Generation) Agentic workflow demos utilizing Box for content storage.

Known for my ability to "get stuff done," I quickly master new technologies and effectively train others. My collaborative approach allows me to work seamlessly across all levels of an organization, from C-Suite executives to individual contributors, to achieve strategic goals.

Key Achievements:

  • Social Media Growth: Drove monthly content impressions up by millions across various platforms as the social media manager.
  • Successful YouTube Series: Produced an AI-focused video series on the Box AI API, achieving tens of thousands of views.
  • Strategic AI Partnerships: Led content partnerships with 10 major AI firms, significantly enhancing product integration and developer resources.
  • Hackathon Leadership: Organized the Box Dev Hackathon, attracting 100 participants and achieving a 95% satisfaction rate.
