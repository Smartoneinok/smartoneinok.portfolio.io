About Me

As Head of Developer Relations & Marketing at Massive (formerly Polygon.io), I kickstarted the developer relations function from scratch, leading a small team while executing marketing campaigns and serving as the internal voice of the developer. I've contributed millions of impressions across social channels to increase brand awareness and influenced customer conversions from free to paid plans.

At Massive, I lead all marketing efforts across the company, oversaw the rebrand from Polygon.io to Massive.com, and completed multiple PR initiatives with NYSE, Nasdaq, TMX, ETF Global, and Benzinga. I independently create demos, write technical blogs, record videos, and manage social media channels to help developers build with fintech data.

Previously, I spent nearly 6 years at Box, rising to Senior Developer Advocate, where I taught developers Box APIs and AI through blogs, videos, sample code, and live demos. I collaborated with major AI partners including OpenAI, Google, Weaviate, and Pinecone, built multiple RAG Agentic workflow demos, and helped code the Box MCP server.

Known for my ability to "get stuff done," I quickly master new technologies and effectively train others. My collaborative approach allows me to work seamlessly across all levels of an organization, from C-Suite executives to individual contributors, to achieve strategic goals.

Key Achievements:

  • Massive Rebrand: Oversaw the planning and completion of the company rebrand from Polygon.io to Massive.com.
  • PR Initiatives: Completed public relations campaigns with NYSE, Nasdaq, TMX, ETF Global, and Benzinga.
  • Social Media Growth: Drove millions of impressions across social platforms at both Massive and Box.
  • Strategic AI Partnerships: Led content partnerships with 10+ major AI firms at Box, enhancing product integration and developer resources.
  • Hackathon Leadership: Organized the Box Dev Hackathon, attracting 100 participants and achieving a 95% satisfaction rate.
